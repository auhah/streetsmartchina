StreetSmartChina is the operating manual for independent travelers who want to land in China with fewer surprises. The launch scaffold is intentionally small: four cluster hubs and one complete sample guide that shows the content pattern.

## Content standard

- Every guide must show when it was last verified.
- Every guide must cite official or primary sources.
- Every guide should include at least one deep module: steps, troubleshooting, a decision table, or a similar practical block.
- Visa and policy pages must stay informational and point readers back to official sources.
